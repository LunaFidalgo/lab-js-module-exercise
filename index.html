Welcome to "Mars Rover" mission

First of all, you have to initialize the grid: you must use the "initialization" function, which receives four arguments: number of obstacles, number of rows, number of columns and number of rovers (1 or 2). .Valid example: initialization(5, 10, 10, 2)
To control the rover you can make the following movements:

  1. Turn the rover: "N" is north. "S" is south. "W" is west and "E" is east. You can do this with: turnleft ("l") or turnright ("r") functions.
  2. Move forward ("f")
  3. Move backwards. ("r")
For this purpose, you can use the function "moveTheRover", which receives what movements you want to do and the rover you want to move ("rover" for rover 1 and "rover2" for rover 2)..
Ah! Remember: the rover is on unknown ground, so... there may be obstacles.
Valid example: moveTheRover("rfffrfffb", rover); moveTheRover("rfffrfffb", rover2)
